About Me

Hi there! My name is Matthew Bronstein, and I'm a 34-year-old web developer from the Chicagoland area. Born in Los Angeles, CA, I moved to Chicago after high school to attend the Chicago College of Performing Arts at Roosevelt University, majoring in French Horn Performance. I graduated in 2010 with a Bachelor's Degree in Music and began my professional career in the world of classical music. For 16 years, I traveled the country and abroad performing with orchestras all over the world, including the symphony orchestras of Chicago, Montreal, North Carolina, Milwaukee, and Richmond. I also had the great privilege of being a member of the critically-acclaimed brass quintet Axiom Brass for 4 years, releasing 2 albums and touring the world performing the music of living composers.

Now, as I embark on a new professional endeavor, I am thrilled to be pursuing a career in web development. There are many cognitive, behavioral, and social similarities between music and coding, including creative problem solving, critical thinking, learning to both lead and work as a team, perserverence, and determination. I look forward to constantly learning new things and working in a professional environment that includes an ever-growing industry and the prospect of never being finished with learning.
